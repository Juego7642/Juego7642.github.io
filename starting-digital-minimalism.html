Starting Digital Minimalism

Finally Breaking Free From The Chains

Approximately on 10th of May I switched to my phone after disabling the browser and the playstore - rendering it a dumbphone

What triggered the change? Having to stay away from the internet for a whole day. I eventually got so bored that I started working out in the shower.

I was quite amazed, especially because I had become aversive to working out. Although I still disapprove of the cancerous culture of youtube fitness, I realized that my aversion to working out resulted from being stimulated by the internet 24/7.

Since I had been reducing my internet usage significantly anyway, it was really easy for me to go cold turkey for two days or so, then I started allowing myself to use some technologies like Whatsapp After 9 pm (ideally). It was the perfect timing, SubhanAllah. I received the oppurtunity to pursue a digital minimalist lifestyle with almost zero roadblocks. I downloaded readera the first night and started to read books to replace compulsive usage - and stopped using the PC entirely. The wisdom of Thoreau and practical knowledge from the book Digital Minimalism filled my free time as I studied for the SSC exams.
